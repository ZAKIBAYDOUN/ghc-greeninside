Quality & Regulatory

Building the Quality System — While Building the Facility

A complete EU-GMP Quality Management System, built from the top down in parallel with construction — saving approximately nine months. Well beyond our Week 1 targets.

EU-GMP Quality Management System Architecture

GHC is not simply documenting operational workflows. We are constructing a pharmaceutical-grade Quality Management System designed for AEMPS inspection from day one. The system is built top-down: corporate governance and data integrity at the foundation, risk-based quality oversight in the middle, and operational control at the execution layer.

Tier 1 — Governance & Data Integrity
Corporate General Log (CGL) — ALCOA+ compliant decision registry. Every corporate decision, workflow change, and regulatory milestone is attributable, legible, contemporaneous, original, accurate, complete, consistent, enduring, and available. 43+ entries logged. This is the audit trail that underpins the entire QMS.
Tier 2 — Quality Oversight
Change Control, CAPA, Deviations, Self-Inspection — the corrective and preventive framework. Risk-based decision making per ICH Q9. Every deviation is captured, investigated, and resolved with root cause analysis. Self-inspection programme ensures continuous readiness.
Tier 3 — Operational Control
19 controlled operational processes spanning document management, training, supplier qualification, complaints handling, product recall, equipment qualification, cleaning validation, environmental monitoring, stability studies, batch records, raw materials, product release, storage, transport, and waste destruction. Each process is designed to EudraLex Volume 4 requirements.
Tier 4 — Validation & Qualification
Equipment IQ/OQ/PQ, cleaning validation, process validation, computer system validation (Annex 11 / GAMP 5) — scheduled to run in parallel with cleanroom commissioning. Validation Master Plan in development. The final gate before AEMPS inspection.
